Extended Reality (XR) and Safety

As Chief of Staff at X Reality Safety Intelligence (XRSI), I have been dedicated to translating a vision for a safer digital future into actionable programs that shape the responsible development of immersive technologies worldwide. My work has been instrumental in establishing XRSI as a leading Standards Developing Organization (SDO) in the immersive technology space (See SDO Listing).

Program Leadership & Impact

Privacy & Safety Framework Development (PSF 2.0)

Contributed to the updated Privacy & Safety Framework 2.0, establishing comprehensive, open-source guidelines aligned with GDPR, NIST, and international standards.

Impact: Created the first globally recognized baseline rulebook for XR and updated guidance for new risks in immersive environments. Read Framework

XRSI Responsible Data Governance (RDG™)

As Project Manager, I led MVP development for the RDG™ program, providing a universal governance framework for organizations operating in XR and AI. The framework introduced the “Human Intelligence In The Loop™” assessment process.

Impact: Pioneered a certification program across healthcare, education, enterprise, and public sectors to mitigate sensitive data risks. Learn More

Child Safety in the Metaverse

Drafting Lead for Standards Australia’s child safety guidance and contributor to XRSI’s Child Safety initiative, ensuring secure digital spaces for children.

Impact: Developed international guidance, contributed to ChildArt Magazine (Metaverse Edition), and influenced safer-by-design principles. Explore Initiative

Medical XR & Digital Health

Worked with the Medical XR Advisory Council (MedXRSI) to create privacy and safety protocols for XR in healthcare, collaborating with JMedXR and industry experts.

Impact: Advanced safety and privacy practices in medical XR deployments and created frameworks for sensitive healthcare data use. Visit MedXRSI

Key Project Leadership & Standards Development

NIST - Natural Authentication for Emergency Management AR Systems

Role: Lead Researcher

Conducted research in collaboration with NIST and the Cyber Battle Federation, developing recommendations for secure and usable authentication mechanisms in high-stakes emergency response AR systems.

Standards Australia - Child Safety Guidance

Role: Drafting Lead

Led drafting efforts and coordinated expert input to create foundational international standards for child safety in immersive environments.
