# Brand Guidelines for Portfolio Website

These guidelines define the **visual identity, style, and reusable design elements** for Bhanujeet Choudharyâ€™s portfolio site. They ensure consistency across all current and future pages.

---

## 1. Colors & Backgrounds
- **Primary Palette (Greyscale Futuristic):**
  - Light Background Gradient: `from-gray-50 via-white to-gray-100`
  - Hero Accent Gradient Dot: `from-gray-400 to-gray-600`
  - Section Card Gradients:
    - AI Journey: `from-gray-400 to-gray-600`
    - XR & Safety: `from-gray-500 to-gray-700`
    - Other Roles: `from-gray-600 to-gray-800`
- **Hover Accents:** Subtle purple-blue highlights
  - Card hover shadow: `rgba(139,92,246,0.5)`
  - Button hover: `from-purple-500 to-blue-500`
- **Text Colors:**
  - Body: `text-gray-700`
  - Headings: Darker shades (`text-gray-800`)
  - Secondary info: `text-gray-600`

---

## 2. Typography
- **Font Family:** Sans-serif (system font stack)
- **Heading Style:**
  - Large, bold, clean (`text-4xl to text-6xl`)
  - Center-aligned for hero and section headers
- **Body Text:**
  - Comfortable readability (`text-lg` for intros, `text-sm` for descriptions)
  - Max width: `max-w-2xl`

---

## 3. Layout & Spacing
- **Sections:**
  - Hero: ~70% viewport height, centered
  - Explore Sections: 3-column grid on desktop, stacked on mobile
  - Contact: Centered with clear CTAs
  - Footer: Small, clean, with credits
- **Padding/Margins:**
  - Vertical spacing: `py-20` to `py-32`
  - Grid gap: `gap-10`

---

## 4. Components
### Hero Section
- Minimal text, background accent dot
- Subdued greyscale tone for sophistication

### Section Cards
- Rounded corners: `rounded-2xl`
- Border: subtle (`border-gray-300`)
- Shadow: soft, with hover glow
- Hover Interaction: scale-up and purple-blue glow

### Buttons / Links
- Rounded full buttons
- Gradient backgrounds (greyscale base, colorful hover)
- Include **icons** for clarity (GitHub, LinkedIn, Email)
- Hover: gradient shift with color pop

### Footer
- Minimal with copyright
- Extra line for credits and external link (e.g., raw information)

---

## 5. Interaction Style
- **Animations:**
  - Hero dot: `animate-pulse`
  - Cards: hover scale + glow
- **Transitions:** Smooth, `transition-colors` or `transition-transform`
- **Hover Colors:** Always shift from neutral greys to subtle accents (purple, blue, pink)

---

## 6. Accessibility
- Ensure text contrast is high (dark text on light backgrounds)
- Buttons should have hover color changes **and** slight shadow/glow changes
- Links: always underlined or clearly styled

---

## 7. Tone & Personality
- **Minimal & Futuristic:** Use space, clean lines, no clutter
- **Professional yet Friendly:** Neutral greys, subtle color pops, approachable CTAs
- **Consistent:** Reuse components and layouts across all pages

---

## 8. Future Page Guidance
When creating new pages (e.g., *AI Journey*, *XR & Safety*, *Other Roles*):
- Use the same **hero structure**: a short intro + accent dot
- Use **consistent headings** (bold, centered)
- Content blocks should follow the same spacing (`py-20`, `px-6`)
- Calls-to-action at the bottom (with consistent button styling)

---

This guideline ensures all future pages stay consistent with the **futuristic greyscale aesthetic** while allowing for subtle, modern hover color accents.
